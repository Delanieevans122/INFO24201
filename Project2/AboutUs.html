About Us: Rent A Pet

Welcome to Rent A Pet! We provide pet lovers with the opportunity to enjoy the companionship of animals without the long-term responsibility of ownership. Read on to learn about our story, where we are today, and what makes us unique!

In the Beginning ...

Rent A Pet began with a family that loved dogs. George Smith and his wife Carol owned two Irish Setters. These dogs were pets to the Smith family children and extended family members. One day as George was walking his dogs in the park, he noticed how they attracted the attention of passing women. They seemed to like to stop what they were doing and ask him about his dogs. A young woman remarked that she would love to have a big dog like that to walk, but she had a small apartment and couldn't keep a large dog. This brief encounter was the beginning of an idea.

What if there were others, possibly many others, who wanted the companionship of pets without the responsibility? Maybe people would be willing to pay to rent or borrow a pet for a short period of time.

Advantages of Renting a Pet:

  • Renters could have access to the pet at their leisure.
  • Renters could pick the pet they wanted.
  • Renters could exercise with the pet.
  • Renters could have companionship of a pet.
  • Renters would not have to pay for upkeep or medical expenses.
  • The owners could have many pets and enjoy them.

Disadvantages of Renting a Pet:

  • The business would need to take care of housing and feeding.
  • The business would need to provide medical expenses.
  • The business would need to have liability insurance.
  • The owners would be tied down with the care of the pets.

Where We Are Today ...

After weighing the pros and cons, the Smiths decided to go ahead and start Rent A Pet in the fall of 1980. The business offered its customers the companionship of a pet without all the care, extra expenses, or hassles of ownership. Initially, the business only provided dogs for rent, but since then, Rent A Pet has expanded its offerings to include cats, horses, pigs, and even snakes.

What Makes Us Unique ...

Rent A Pet has expanded from its early location in Provo and now has franchises in Logan, Bountiful, Salt Lake City, and St. George. We employ our own trained vet and have yet to experience any accidents with our rental pets. We also have an animal trainer who works with the dogs and horses daily.
